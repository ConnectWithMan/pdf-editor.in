Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 26, 2024

1. Introduction

At PDF Editor, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our website and services.

2. File Security and Retention

When you upload files to our service for editing, they are transferred securely using SSL encryption. We do not read or analyze the content of your files. All uploaded files and processed documents are automatically deleted from our servers after a short period (typically within a few hours) to ensure your privacy.

3. Data Collection

We do not require you to create an account to use our basic services. We may collect anonymous usage data (such as browser type, time of visit, and pages viewed) to help us improve our website performance and user experience.

4. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your experience on our site. Cookies are small text files stored on your device. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect some website functionality.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services (such as Google Analytics) to analyze website traffic. These services may collect information about your use of the site in accordance with their own privacy policies.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at https://www.famral.com/contact.
